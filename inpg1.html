Nighttime Smokey Eyes

Published: Septmeber 21st, 2017

Do you want to change up your nighttime makeup routine? Well a smoky eye is a dramatic style that will take peoples breath away. By creating the perfect light to dark ratio you can make your eyes pop. This look has an edgy feel, one look at someone could stop them cold. It creates a more dynamic flow than just putting on one regular shade of eyeshadow.

Materials

  • Good Ligthing
  • Mirror
  • Eyeshadow Primer
  • Eyeshadow Burshes
  • Eyeshadow Palette
  • Mascara
  • Eyeliner

Steps

  1. Apply eyeshadow primer all over lids
  2. Set a base of a neutral color
  3. Above the crease of the lid use medium toned shades as an accent
  4. Then add a deep shade by the lash line, blending towards the crease
  5. Along the inner corners, brow bone, and waterline add lighter shades as a highlight
  6. Apply winged eyeliner
  7. Apply mascara
